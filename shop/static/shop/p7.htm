Product description

Colour:Mysterious Blue

Plug Into Nirvana with boAt Airdopes 171 True Wireless earbuds to enjoy your playlist in a truly wireless way. You can tap into 3 times the charge with its 380mAh carry cum charging case that offers an additional playtime of up to 10 hours. With a battery capacity of 43mAh for each earbud, indulge in blissful audio experience for up to 3 hours in a go. It has the IPX4 rated shield to offers resistance against water and dust scares. The TWS Airdopes 171 come equipped with 6mm drivers for immersive sound. The stereo calling feature lets the user indulge in smooth and clear chats over calls. Its latest Bluetooth v5.0 offers seamless instant connectivity. One can access the instant voice assistant by just a single press via the Multifunction Button control. The ergonomically designed Airdopes 171 come in a dual tone finish for a premium presence
